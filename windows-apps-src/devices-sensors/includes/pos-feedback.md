- 在 MSDN UWP 論壇上的文章中包含["pointofservice"](https://social.msdn.microsoft.com/Forums/en-US/home?forum=wpdevelop&filter=alltypes&sort=relevancedesc&searchTerm=%5Bpointofservice%5D)詞彙
